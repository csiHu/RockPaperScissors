About This Game

This is a simple Rock Paper Scissors game.

Game Rules

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple game where:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

The player chooses one of the three options, and the computer randomly chooses another. The winner is determined based on the rules above. If both choices are the same, it's a draw.

Game Strategy

While Rock Paper Scissors is based on luck, players can increase their chances by understanding some psychological patterns:

  • Start with Rock: Many players begin with Rock because it's perceived as the "strong" choice. If you start with Paper, you might win more often.
  • Watch for patterns: Players may repeat their winning choices or avoid losing ones. Try to predict their next move based on this.
